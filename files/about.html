About

Laurent Laval

Leadership Advisory — Creator of Reason for Being by Laurent Laval™

Laurent Laval began his career on assembly lines in France and Germany, gaining first-hand experience of industrial environments and the people who drive organizational success.

An engineering graduate from ISARA, he went on to hold international leadership, training, and development roles with Manitou, New Holland, and Bobcat Doosan. There, he created the EMEA Academy, a platform dedicated to developing technical, managerial, and leadership capabilities across Europe.

Since 2020, Laurent has been Managing Partner of Arthur Hunt Czech Republic. He advises leaders and executive teams on leadership, executive search, and organizational transformation.

He also facilitates forums that bring together CEOs and HR leaders to explore the human challenges facing organizations.

Laurent is the creator of the Reason for Being by Laurent Laval™ methodology.
